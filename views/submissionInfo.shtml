Submission Guidelines

Research from any discipline (natural sciences, social sciences, humanities, etc.) is eligible for publication in the Maryland Undergraduate Research Journal (MURJ).


Eligibility

  • Primary author must be a University of Maryland undergraduate, or a recent graduate (≤1 year) of the University of Maryland.
  • Submissions must contain original research conducted by a University of Maryland undergraduate.
  • Submissions may not have been previously published in another research journal.
  • Submissions must be approved for publication in MURJ by a mentor (principal investigator, faculty member, etc.).
  • Mentors may be listed as secondary authors.

Formatting

  • Submissions should fall within 2,000 to 4,000 words in length; submissions that fall outside this range will also be considered.
  • Any relevant figures/tables should be included in the submission, and labeled appropriately.
  • Submissions should be a single file; separate attachments will not be considered without prior approval.
  • Submissions must be in one of the following file formats: DOC, DOCX.
  • Successful submissions will be formatted by MURJ prior to publication.

Structure

  • Submissions do not need to follow any particular structure; however, submissions should be structured similarly to other research articles.
  • Submissions should include an abstract as a brief summary; abstracts will not be included in the word count.
  • Submissions should follow APA style for any references, citations, etc.

Submission Deadline

MURJ has no submission deadline; submissions are accepted on a rolling basis.


Questions?

If you have questions about any of the above Submission Guidelines, please email us at umd.murj@gmail.com
