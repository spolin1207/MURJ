A Call for Submissions!

08/29/2022


We are excited to announce that the Maryland Undergraduate Research Journal is now accepting submissions! Any original research conducted by a University of Maryland undergraduate is eligible for publication in MURJ; please refer to our Submission Guidelines for more information. Publishing with MURJ offers several advantages over traditional research journals, including increased accessibility for undergraduates and a greater variety of research accepted. In addition, a $150 prize will be awarded to the author of the Top Manuscript, as determined by our staff. We eagerly await your submission!
